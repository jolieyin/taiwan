About Taiwan

Let's get to know about Taiwan...

TAIWAN

Taiwan is a country in East Asia with the population of nearly 23.6 million.
The main island has an area of 35,808 square kilometers (13,826 sq mi).
“Taipei” is the capital as well as the largest metropolitan area of Taiwan.

Government

- NT$
